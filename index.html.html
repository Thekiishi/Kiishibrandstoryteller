About

Copy That Converts.
Strategy That Sticks.

I'm Kiishi. A conversion copywriter and marketing strategist for real estate agencies, developers, and the brand owners who want to stop blending in.

Listing copy that reads like a feature dump is one of the most expensive mistakes in property marketing. Buyers don't connect with square footage. They connect with how a space makes them feel.

I write copy that leads with the feeling and backs it up with the facts. Then, because great copy sitting in a drawer helps no one, I set up your ads on Meta and get it in front of the right people, fast.
