The Work

See the Difference
Copy Makes.

Most property copy describes. Good property copy transports. Here's what that looks like in practice.

// Luxury Apartment, City Centre
Before

2-bedroom apartment available in the city centre. Open-plan living space with floor-to-ceiling windows. Modern kitchen with integrated appliances. Master bedroom with en-suite. Underground parking included. Close to public transport and local amenities. Available for immediate occupation. EPC rating B.

After

On the fourteenth floor, the city becomes something you look at rather than something you're in. You're close enough to be part of everything, far enough up that none of it follows you through the door. The apartment is open where it should be and private where it matters. The kitchen is fitted for someone who actually uses one. The windows are not a feature, they're the reason. And when you leave in the morning, the whole city is already waiting for you at ground level, with parking ready when you come back up.

// Four-Bedroom Family Home, Suburbs
Before

Spacious four-bedroom detached house in a quiet residential area. Large garden to the rear. Double garage. Newly fitted kitchen and bathrooms. Good school catchment area. Off-street parking for three vehicles. Close to parks and green spaces. Chain-free sale. Viewings by appointment.

After

You haven't stepped inside yet and you're already measuring the driveway with your eyes. Four bedrooms means no one has to share. The garden is the kind that fills up naturally, with a trampoline, a table, a pile of things that belong outside. The school situation is why half the road moved here. The other half came because someone told them how quiet the evenings are, and found out they were right.

// Commercial Unit, Mixed-Use Development
Before

Ground-floor commercial unit available within a new mixed-use development. 1,200 sq ft of flexible retail or office space. Full glass frontage. High footfall location. Suitable for F&B, retail, or professional services. Lease terms negotiable. Available Q1. Enquiries to the agent.

After

This unit sits exactly where foot traffic lands before it decides where to go. Ground floor, full glass frontage, 1,200 square feet of space that puts your brand in direct sight of everyone moving through the development. The people who will become regulars here haven't found their habits yet because the neighbourhood is still forming. That's the point. The businesses that move in now get to be part of what this place becomes, not a late addition to something already established. Flexible lease terms. Available Q1.

The Principle

Features tell.
Stories sell.

Every buyer makes an emotional decision first and justifies it with logic later. The agencies and developers who understand this are the ones who stop chasing leads and start attracting them.
